⚠ Multi-GPU Clustering Required
Your workload requires multiple GPUs. Your systems team must configure a parallelism framework such as vLLM, DeepSpeed, or Hugging Face Accelerate using Tensor Parallelism to split the model across GPU interconnects.
⚠ NVLink Interconnect Required
With multiple GPUs required, PCIe bandwidth will bottleneck inference throughput. Specify servers with NVLink / NVSwitch fabric (e.g. DGX H100, HGX H200) in your vendor RFQ. PCIe-only multi-GPU rigs will deliver significantly lower tokens/sec.
⚠ Power & Cooling Warning
Estimated peak draw exceeds 2 kW. Your server room needs dedicated 220V–240V circuits, a matching UPS, and adequate BTU air conditioning or liquid cooling. Include this in your facilities request before placing the hardware order.
⚠ Hardware Scale Limit Exceeded
This workload exceeds a single standard 8-GPU on-prem node. Consider INT4 quantization to reduce requirements, or consult an OEM provider such as Supermicro or Dell Enterprise AI for a custom multi-node cluster.
⚠ UAE BIS Export Control
H100/H200 GPUs are US-controlled (BIS Tier 2). Without Validated End-User (VEU) status, allow 8–24 additional weeks for export licensing. Engage Ingram Micro Gulf or Redington Gulf as authorized channels. Start compliance process 6 months ahead.
